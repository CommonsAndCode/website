AI is moving fast, and in municipal politics and administration many people are struggling to keep up with the pace of change.

We want to clarify: **How can we tell useful AI applications from hype in practice?**<br>
We want to shape: **How should AI be used at municipal level?**<br>
We want to learn: **How are you already using AI in your civic engagement?**

This event is neither techno-optimism nor doom rhetoric.
It is an invitation to a practical and honest discussion beyond buzzwords.

**Please note: this event is primarily aimed at a German-speaking audience.**
Most talks, moderation, and discussion rounds will take place in German.

☕ Refreshments are provided by the association.
