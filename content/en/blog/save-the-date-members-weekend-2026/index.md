Dear Members, 

The Member Assembly will take place on the weekend of September 26 & 27, 2026 in Germany.

We are planning a relatively short assembly that will take place on just one day, most likely Saturday.

We are also planning other activities and a public event that weekend. 

Further planning is now underway, and more information will follow. Please feel free to contact us with any requests, ideas, or questions: [contact@commons-and-code.eu](mailto:contact@commons-and-code.eu).
